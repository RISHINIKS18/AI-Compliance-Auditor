================================================================================
SECURITY BEST PRACTICES
================================================================================

To maintain data security:

✓ Use strong, unique passwords for all systems
✓ Enable multi-factor authentication on all accounts
✓ Lock your computer when stepping away from your desk
✓ Report suspicious emails or security incidents immediately
✓ Complete annual security training (due by February 28)

✗ Do not use personal email for work communications
✗ Do not store work data on personal devices
✗ Do not share login credentials with colleagues
✗ Do not use public WiFi for accessing customer data
✗ Do not take screenshots of customer information

================================================================================
INCIDENT REPORTING
================================================================================

If you suspect a security incident or data breach:

1. Report immediately to: security@techcorp.com
2. Call the security hotline: 1-800-SECURE-1
3. Do not attempt to investigate on your own
4. Preserve all evidence (do not delete emails or logs)
5. Document what you observed

All reports are confidential and non-retaliatory.
